Training and development activities are recorded by the postgraduate researcher in the following ways:

h2. Analysis

Provides a training needs analysis which allows level and priorities to be set, with links to training and development events.

[IMAGE training-needs-f.png Training needs analysis]

h2. Training plan

Depending on the institutional set up, this can be submitted annually and approved by the supervisor, or can be submitted throughout the academic year.

[IMAGE training-plan-f.png Training plan]

h2. Log

Postgraduate researchers are invited to institutional events and can respond via the system.

[IMAGE training-event-f.png RSVP event]

These events are logged and the attendance record displayed.

External events can be added by postgraduate researchers.

The log can be downloaded.

[IMAGE training-log-f.png Development Log]

h2. Calendar

Future events are visible via the calendar.

[IMAGE training-calendar-f.png Calendar]
